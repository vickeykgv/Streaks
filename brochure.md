## What makes it different

Five things set this app apart from the dozens of single-purpose trackers out there.

### 🔒 Local-first by design
Your data is written to your device *first*, always. The app is fully functional the moment you open it — **no sign-up wall, no onboarding survey, no email required.** An account is something you add later only if you want sync. Most apps treat your data as their product. Here, it's simply yours.

### 📴 Genuinely offline
This isn't "offline-ish." Every screen, every form, every report works with the network switched completely off — on a flight, in a parking garage, on a mountain road. It's built as a true offline-first Progressive Web App, so there are no spinners waiting on a server and no "you're offline" dead-ends.

### 📲 Installs like a native app
Add it to your home screen on **iPhone, Android, Windows, or Mac**. It launches full-screen with its own icon, loads instantly, and quietly updates itself in the background — gently letting you know when a new version is ready.

### 🔄 Sync only if you want it
Create an account and your habits, transactions, and vehicle logs follow you across every device automatically. Sync is **opt-in, bidirectional, and conflict-resilient** — edit on your phone offline, edit on your laptop, and the app reconciles them cleanly when you reconnect. Sign out any time; your local data stays put.

### 🎨 One calm, consistent design
Three modules, one design language. Light, dark, and system themes. Friendly empty states that guide you instead of staring back blankly. Smooth animations that respect "reduce motion." And an always-present floating **quick-add button** so capturing something new is never more than a tap away.

## Module 1 — Habits & Tasks
### Build the routines you want. Hit the deadlines you set. Watch the streaks grow.

This is where daily life gets organized. Track **recurring habits** and **one-off tasks** in the same place, with a dashboard engineered around one question: *what do I need to do today?*

#### Five ways to measure — because not every habit is a checkbox
| You want to… | The app gives you… | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Just do it | A single-tap checkbox | "Take vitamins" |
| Hit a number | A +/− counter against a target | "Drink 8 glasses of water" |
| Put in the time | A start/stop timer + manual entry | "Meditate 20 minutes" |
| Log a value | A number input | "Morning weigh-in (kg)" |
| Track a feeling | A 1–5 rating picker | "Mood today" |

Each habit automatically shows the right control on your dashboard, so logging is instant and obvious.

#### A dashboard that prioritizes for you
Open the app and your day is already sorted into sections, in the order that matters:
- **Overdue** — tasks past their due date, so nothing slips.
- **Today's Habits** — only the ones actually scheduled for today.
- **Today's Tasks** — due today, still pending.
- **Upcoming** — a 7-day look-ahead so tomorrow isn't a surprise.
- **Completed today** — collapsed at the bottom, with one-tap undo if you tick something by mistake.

The common case — open app, tick off today's items — is designed to take **as little as two taps.**

#### Personal & Professional, kept separate
A simple tab switch splits your **Personal** life from your **Professional** one. Filter the whole dashboard to either world, and your choice is remembered next time.

#### Scheduling that bends to your life
- Habits: **daily, specific weekdays** (Mon/Wed/Fri), or **every N days.**
- Tasks: **due dates, due times, and Low / Medium / High priority.**
- Optional start and end dates so seasonal habits don't linger.

#### See your progress, not just your to-dos
- **Current and longest streaks** on every habit.
- A **90-day completion heatmap** so you can spot patterns at a glance.
- **Completion rates** over 7 days, 30 days, or all-time.

#### Organize it your way
Tags with autocomplete, custom colors, and icons. Filter, search, and sort your habit and task lists by name, streak, completion rate, due date, or priority. Archive what you've outgrown without losing the history.

#### Reminders that actually fire
Opt in to **real OS-level push notifications** that arrive at the correct local time — even when the app is closed, even after you change time zones. Act straight from the notification with **"Mark done"** or **"Snooze."** (Permission is only ever requested when *you* set a reminder — never on launch.)

## Module 2 — Spending Tracker
### Understand where your money goes — without a spreadsheet or a bank login.

A complete personal-finance module for income, expenses, and transfers across all your accounts. You enter what you spend; the app turns it into clarity.

#### All your accounts in one place
Cash, bank accounts, credit cards, wallets, and more — each with its own opening balance, color, and icon. See balances at a glance and keep your whole financial picture in a single view.

#### Three transaction types, done right
- **Expenses** — with category, payee, note, and tags.
- **Income** — track every source.
- **Transfers** — move money between your own accounts cleanly, without double-counting it as spending.

#### Categories that match how you think
Dedicated income and expense categories with **sub-categories**, each with its own icon and color. Need a looser grouping? Add tags on top. Categories keep your reports meaningful; tags keep them flexible.

#### Budgets that keep you honest
Set **weekly, monthly, yearly, or custom-period** budgets with **live progress bars.** Turn on **rollover** to carry unspent amounts forward. You'll always know how much runway is left before month-end.

#### Recurring transactions on autopilot
Set up rent, salary, EMIs, and subscriptions **once.** The app logs them automatically on their due dates so your records stay complete with zero manual effort.

#### Reports that reveal the story
- **Spending trends** over time.
- **Category pie charts** that show where the money really goes.
- **Cashflow** views balancing income against expenses.

All rendered as clean, interactive charts.

#### Find any transaction in seconds
Filter your history by **date range, type, account, category, tags, amount, or free-text search** — and combine them. The filtered view is even **shareable by URL.**

#### Your money, your file
- **Full JSON export & import** for complete backups.
- **CSV export** of transactions for taxes, reimbursement, or your accountant.
- Pick your **base currency** in Settings (defaults to ₹ INR).

## Module 3 — Moto Logging
### The complete logbook for everyone who owns a vehicle.

Stop scattering your vehicle's life across a notes app, a glovebox full of receipts, and forgotten SMS reminders. Moto puts it in one place and answers the questions that matter: ***What's my real cost per kilometre? When is the next service due? What still needs fixing before my next visit?***

#### A garage, not just a single vehicle
Add as many vehicles as you own — **bikes, cars, scooters, and more** — each with make, model, year, registration, fuel type, and tank capacity. A quick **vehicle switcher** scopes every screen to the one you're looking at.

#### Fuel logging with automatic efficiency
Record each fill-up — litres, price, odometer, station, full-tank or partial — and the app **computes your real-world fuel efficiency (km/L) automatically** from your fill history. No mental math, no spreadsheet.

#### Service & maintenance history that looks ahead
Log every service with **labor cost, parts cost, workshop, and odometer reading** — then set the **next-due date or next-due odometer** so the app warns you *before* it's overdue. Your full maintenance record, always at hand for resale or warranty.

#### Spare-parts tracker
Record part replacements with cost and **expected life** (in km or months). The app shows a **"due soon" badge** as a part nears the end of its life, so a worn chain or aging battery never catches you off guard.

#### Issues — your running fix-it list
Jot down problems the moment you notice them on the road, set a **Low / Medium / High** priority, and tick them off — even **linking an issue to the service that resolved it** for a clean paper trail.

#### Notes per vehicle
Free-form, **pinnable** notes for anything that doesn't fit a form — tyre pressures, preferred oil grade, mechanic's phone number.

#### Document expiry, never missed
Track **insurance and driving-license** expiry with clear **Valid / Expiring / Expired** status chips and a **push reminder** a configurable number of days before they lapse. No more last-minute scrambles or lapsed cover.

#### Analytics that pay for themselves
- **Cost per kilometre** — your true running cost.
- **Monthly spend split** across fuel, service, parts, and other.
- **Fuel-efficiency trend** line to catch a developing problem early.
- **Service-interval timeline** to see your maintenance rhythm.

#### Metric or imperial
Switch units to suit you; everything is stored consistently under the hood, so your numbers always add up.

## Privacy & ownership

In an era where every app wants your data, this one is deliberately different:

- **No account required.** Use every feature, forever, without signing up.
- **Local-first storage.** Your data lives in your browser/device, not on someone's server by default.
- **Opt-in sync only.** Nothing leaves your device until you choose to enable an account.
- **Sign out keeps your data.** Logging out stops syncing — it never wipes your local records.
- **Full export, any time.** Take everything with you as JSON (or CSV for transactions). **No lock-in, ever.**

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. The app is fully functional offline with no sign-up. An account is optional and only enables cross-device sync.

**Does it connect to my bank?**
No. The Spending Tracker is manual-entry by design — which is exactly why it's private and works offline. Nothing of yours is shared with a bank or third party.

**Will it work without internet?**
Yes, completely. It's built offline-first as a Progressive Web App. Network is only ever used for optional sync and reminders.

**Can I use it on both my phone and laptop?**
Yes. Install it on each device, sign in, and your data syncs across all of them automatically.

**Can I get my data out?**
Always. Export everything as JSON at any time, plus CSV for transactions. There's no lock-in.

**Will reminders interrupt me on launch?**
Never. Notification permission is only requested at the moment *you* set a reminder — and you can use the whole app without enabling them at all.
